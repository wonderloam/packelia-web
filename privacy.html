Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Packelia ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal packing assistant. This policy describes what we collect, why, and how it's stored. We aim to collect only what's necessary to make the app work.

What We Collect

  • Email address — used solely to send you a magic sign-in link. We do not send marketing email.
  • Wardrobe content — clothing item photos, categories, tags, and notes you create. Photos are stored as image files; categories and tags are structured data.
  • Trip data — trip name (optional), destination, dates, packing checklist items, and outfit assignments you create.
  • Profile preferences — display name, avatar (optional), temperature unit preference, onboarding completion state.
  • Subscription state — whether your account has an active Pro subscription. Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play; we do not see or store payment card details.
  • Anonymized usage analytics — screen views and feature usage events, tied to your account ID. Disabled in development builds. No raw input content (clothing photos, item names, trip names) is sent to analytics.
  • Push notification token — only if you grant notification permission, used to send trip reminders.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not access your device location. Weather forecasts are based only on the destination you type into a trip, geocoded via a public API.
  • We do not access your photo library beyond the images you explicitly select to upload.
  • We do not access your camera except when you tap to take a clothing photo.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, or other apps.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers.

How Your Data Is Stored

Account data, wardrobe entries, trips, outfits, and packing lists are stored in our Supabase project (PostgreSQL database, hosted on AWS infrastructure). Every table enforces row-level security so that records are visible only to the account that owns them. Clothing photos are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket scoped to your user ID.

Sessions are persisted on your device using the OS's secure storage. We do not store passwords because we do not use passwords — sign-in is by magic link only.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — hosts the database, file storage, and authentication. Supabase privacy policy.
  • Open-Meteo — public weather API. We send a destination's latitude/longitude (geocoded from the city you type) to retrieve forecasts. No account info is sent. Open-Meteo terms.
  • PostHog — anonymized product analytics, used to understand which features are used. PostHog privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription state across iOS and Android. Receives your account ID and subscription status. RevenueCat privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handles payment processing for subscriptions and distributes the app.

Trip Sharing

You can invite other people to collaborate on a trip by sharing an invite link. When someone joins your trip, they can see the trip's destination, dates, outfits, and packing list according to the permissions you set. Your wardrobe items remain private to your account; only items you've assigned to a shared trip's outfits become visible to invitees.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's settings, or by emailing support@wonderloam.com. Deletion removes your records from our database and storage; backups are rotated out within 30 days.

You can request a copy of your data by emailing the same address.

Children's Privacy

Packelia is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email.

Contact

Questions? Email support@wonderloam.com.
